This is a checklist you should go through before the Midterm Presentation. On the day of presentation, you will also follow this same list to take the audience through your project.

During presentation, everyelse listens carefully, which means LIDDOWN, SCREENSHUT on all personal electronic devices.

After each presenter finishes, the whole class claps and congratuate the presenter.

A QA session follows, where the rest of the audience test the site and offer additional feedback.

Overall Design

  1. What's is this design project about?
  2. How you want your audience to feel?
  3. Did you design a logo and/or tagline? What's the inspiration?
  4. Did you use full-size decorative images? Why you chose these images?
  5. What's the main color, ambience color? Why you chose them?

Information Architecture

  1. How many pages do you have and why?
  2. How did you decide on what information goes on which page? Did you make any adjustment? Why?
  3. On each page, what's the most important and less important information? How does your design help audience understand your intention?

Navigation Bar

  1. Appropriate positioning on all pages.
  2. Consistent padding across all desktop pages
  3. Collapse on mobile devices
  4. Appropriate margin from other elements on the same page
  5. When colapsing to iPad and Mobile sizes, not blocked by any other content on the page

Text

  1. Header, Tagline, Body Text size clearly reflect information hierarchy.
  2. Font weight toned down on iphone/ipads

Sectioning

  1. Appropriate spacing between sections
  2. No wierd padding/margin issues on left/right side of pages.

Portfolio Page

  1. Multi-culumn on desktop, reduced down to single column on cellphone
  2. Appropriate spacing between images
  3. CSS filter and JQuery filter effects on individual images on hover
  4. Modal effect on individual images on click

Contact Page

  1. Clickable mailto link that opens up a googlemail template onclick.
  2. Embedded Google Map with custom address

Optional Checklist

  1. Customized Google Map
  2. Animation triggered by Scrolling
  3. Anything else you want to play with, surprise us.
