Follow the steps below to make a pattern in Photoshop

  1. Google a pattern and save it on your desktop
  2. Open the pattern in Photoshop. Go to EDIT ==> Define Pattern on the top menu.
  3. Right click the layer you want to apply the pattern on, and click Pattern Overlay.
