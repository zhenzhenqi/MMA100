Technical Reference

How to repeat duplicate lines/shapes in Photoshop.
  1. Draw a line/shape.
  2. Hold Alt+Command+T and drag the line to where you want, hit Enter.
  3. Then hold Shift+Alt+Command+T to duplicate.
How to convert shape into path and turn it into mask layer.
  1. Draw a shape.
  2. Hold Command key and click this shape's icon in Layers. You will now see dashed lines on the edge of the shape.
  3. Select another layers you wish to cut in Layers, and select the Add Layer Mask icon right under the Layers area.
  4. What is outside of the edge of the shape will now be invisible.
